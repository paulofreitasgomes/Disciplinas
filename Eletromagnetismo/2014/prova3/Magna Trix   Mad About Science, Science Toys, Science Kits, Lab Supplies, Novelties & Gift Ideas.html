MAGNETISM

Magna Trix

Discover the mystery of magnetism with this modern classic. Three plastic-encased ceramic magnets will float, roll and seem to defy gravity. Simply thread the brightly coloured magnet rings onto the spindle, and watch them hover mysteriously in air. A must for demonstrating the fundamentals of repulsion in magnestism.

Age: 6+ with adult supervision
What You Get
Magna-Trix presented in an attractive transparent case. Instructions included.
How does it work?
When the magnets are threaded onto the spindle with opposite poles facing each other, repulsion causes the magnets to hover and float in air. A memorable way to demonstrate attraction and repulsion forces in magnetism. The magnets are encased in plastic to resist breakage.
Teach
  • Principals of magnetism
  • Magnetic attraction and repulsion forces
